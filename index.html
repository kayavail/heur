Frontend Designer / Developer - working student or intern (m / f / d)

We are Heureka Prints a small e-commerce startup and produce personalized posters. The company started two years ago as a side project to our studies. We are now the market leader in our niche with Nachthimmel.shop and The Star Poster in several countries. We want to build several new products next year and we need your help.

Your tasks:

  • You design elements of our pages , e.g. parts of an editor or a landing page
  • You keep an eye on the customer perspective: What do our customers want? How can we make our site more usable?
  • You implement smaller features with a strong focus on design and usability

Your profile:

  • You are studying something relevant (e.g. design or computer science)
  • You have at least 10 hours a week
  • You can develop simple things with HTML, CSS and Javascript
  • You have experience with relevant design programs
  • You speak German or English. Any other European language is a big plus
  • You like to work independently and take initiative

Why Eureka Prints?

We are a small development team, with us you see everything and work a little on everything . If you are actually interested in everything related to a small software / e-commerce startup, you have come to the right place. You can take a look at everything, we are always happy about initiatives. If you want to start a company yourself in the long term , we would be happy to design your job so that you can gain initial experience here.

We are currently working in a co-working space in Berlin, you are also welcome to work remotely.

Interested?

Send to daniel@heurekaprints.com 1) a few sentences about yourself 2) a resume, 3) some code that you have written or something that you have designed.
